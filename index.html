Dark Fantasy Action Roguelike

Survive the waves. Adapt your build. Become legendary. A dark fantasy action roguelike with wave-based combat and a 3/4 top-down perspective. Each run is a new attempt to survive hordes of dark creatures — and every defeat makes you stronger.

Features

⚔

Intense Combat

Face increasingly dangerous waves of enemies, culminating in brutal bosses. Combine automatic and active skills to create devastating combos — melee, ranged, area or single-target.

⚙

Build Your Way

32 skills split between physical and magical. Equipment with damage bonuses and trophies with unique effects. The Blacksmith transmutes your gear to adapt to any strategy.

🏢

Preparation Room

Between each wave, visit the Merchant to buy equipment and trophies, or the Blacksmith to transmute your items. Gear up before facing the next challenge.

⬆

Permanent Progression

Ashes collected during runs unlock upgrades at the Altar that persist between matches. The more you play, the stronger your next hero starts.

👑

12 Unique Heroes

Each hero has a unique starting skill, passive and stats — from brutal berserkers to arcane sorceresses. Find the one that fits your style.

🎲

Free Demo

Try a selection of heroes and challenges for free. The full game brings even more content, characters and difficulties.
